About Dr. Lee

Dr. Christine Lee is a board‑certified rheumatologist dedicated to helping patients with autoimmune diseases live active, fulfilling lives. A lifelong ballet dancer, she understands the importance of mobility and the impact when pain or stiffness gets in the way. Her empathy is shaped by personal experience—her father contracted polio as a child—giving her unique insight into the challenges of chronic illness.

Beyond arthritis and autoimmune care, Dr. Lee focuses on reproductive immunology, guiding patients through fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum health when autoimmune conditions are involved. She combines advanced training from Stanford University with a warm, personalized approach, and serves as co‑investigator on numerous clinical trials while speaking nationally on lupus, arthritis, and patient care.
